# Lean Canvas

## Problem

- Travelers can become medical emergencies away from their home system.
- Clinicians often need a short emergency summary, not a full records integration.
- Patients need confidence that emergency access is narrow, temporary, and auditable.

## Customer segments

- international travelers with chronic conditions, serious allergies, implants, or anticoagulants
- hospital international patient desks
- urgent care and emergency departments seeing visitors or inbound tourists
- travel insurers and medical assistance coordinators

## Unique value proposition

MedAgent gives the right emergency summary to the right clinician for the right amount of time, with a visible agent workflow and a Solana-backed audit trail.

## Solution

- locally encrypted emergency summary
- deterministic 3-tier access policy
- patient approval pause/resume for semi-trusted clinicians
- Solana-backed audit logging with an auditor view that shows slot and transaction signature
- LLM translation and clinician briefing constrained to the authorized subset only

## Channels

- hospital innovation teams
- travel insurance partners
- airport and destination health networks
- expatriate, student, and corporate travel programs

## Revenue

- B2B SaaS per covered traveler
- enterprise seat + volume pricing for hospital groups
- bundled audit/compliance add-on for insurers or assistance desks

## Cost structure

- application hosting and storage
- OpenAI usage for translation and follow-up answers
- Solana transaction costs for audit writes
- customer onboarding and compliance review

## Key metrics

- number of travelers enrolled
- number of emergency sessions opened
- time from request to authorized summary
- percent of requests resolved through Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3, or denial
- auditor page usage and review completion

## Unfair advantage

- visible, judge-friendly combination of agentic workflow plus ledger-backed auditability
- no PHI on-chain, which keeps the design credible
- clearer emergency-only scope than a general medical records platform
